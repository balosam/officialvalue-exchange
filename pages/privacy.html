1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to process your trade and provide you with support. This includes:

  • Contact information — your name, phone number, and email address when you reach out via WhatsApp, our contact form, or live chat.
  • Transaction information — the type and value of assets you wish to trade (e.g. gift card denomination, cryptocurrency amount), and your Nigerian bank account details necessary to process your payment.

We do not collect government-issued ID, BVN, NIN, passwords, or any biometric data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve our services:

  • To verify your trade request and process payment to your bank account.

  • To provide customer support via WhatsApp, email, and live chat.

  • To improve our website and service quality based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

We will never use your information for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes — ever.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:
